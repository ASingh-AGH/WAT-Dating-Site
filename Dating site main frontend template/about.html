Our mission is to help you find your perfect match in a fun and easy way. Cherii is a dating site that connects you with compatible singles based on your preferences, personality, and lifestyle. Whether you are looking for a serious relationship, a casual fling, or just a friend to chat with, Cherii has someone for you.

Cherii is more than just a dating site. It is a community of like-minded people who share your interests and passions. You can browse profiles, send messages, and get ready for exciting dates and activites. You can also contact our developers and support teams for help as we have a strict anti bullying policy. Cherii is here to support you on your journey to love and happiness.

Cherii was founded in 2023 by a team of four students who wanted to create a better way to meet new people online. They were inspired by their own experiences and challenges in finding love and friendship in the digital age. They decided to build a platform that would combine the best features of social media, dating apps, and matchmaking services.

Cherii launched in 2024 and quickly gained popularity among singles who were looking for a more authentic and personalized way to connect. Since then, Cherii has grown to become one of the leading dating sites in the world, with millions of members from different countries and backgrounds. Cherii has also won several awards and recognitions for its innovation and quality. Cherii is proud to be part of many success stories and happy couples who met through our site.

We are Cherii, a team of passionate and dedicated professionals who love what we do. We are committed to creating a safe, fun, and effective dating site that helps you find your ideal partner. We are also constantly improving and innovating our platform to offer you the best features and services.
